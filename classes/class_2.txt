Class: Dish

Attributes:
color (string)
totalNumberOfDishes (integer)
brokeDish (boolean)
clean (boolean)

Methods:

brokenDish (subtracts 1 from totalNumberOfDishes and change brokeDish to true)
buyTenMoreDishes (Adds ten to totalNumberOfDishes attribute)
cleanAllDishes (change clean attribute)
