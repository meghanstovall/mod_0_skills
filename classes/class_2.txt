Class: Dishes

Attributes:
color (string)
totalNumberOfDishes (integer)
numberOfDirtyDishes (integer)
allDishesClean (boolean)

Methods:
changeColor (changes the color attribute)
calculateNumberOfCleanDishes (subtracts numberOfDirtyDishes from totalNumberOfDishes)
cleanedTenDishes (subtracts 10 from numberOfDirtyDishes)
cleanAllDishes (change allDishesClean attribute and change numberOfDirtyDishes to 0)
